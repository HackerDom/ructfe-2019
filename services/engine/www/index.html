ENGINE /

An engine is a part of your submarine designed to convert heat energy into mechanical energy. Like any heat engine it burns a fuel to work.

FUEL /

A fuel is any material that can be burned in an engine so that it releases energy in the form of heat energy. Heat energy is converted into mechanical energy and makes the submarine sail in the ocean.

PROPERTY /

A property is some characteristic of the type of fuel. You can check any properties of your fuel to better represent your engine and increase fuel efficiency.
